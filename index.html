Seven Wonders of the World

Great Wall of China

The Great Wall is a series of fortifications made of various materials and built along the northern borders of China to protect against invasions.

Petra

Petra, a historical and archaeological city in Jordan, is famous for its rock-cut architecture and water conduit system.

Christ the Redeemer

The Christ the Redeemer statue is a 98-foot-tall statue of Jesus Christ in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, overlooking the city from the Corcovado Mountain.

Colosseum

The Colosseum, located in the center of Rome, Italy, is an ancient amphitheater known for gladiatorial contests and public spectacles.

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, India, is a white marble mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu is a 15th-century Inca citadel located in the Eastern Andes in Peru, renowned for its dry-stone construction and panoramic views.
